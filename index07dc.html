Niche Design Loft is a turnkey interior design startup set up in 2017 by Karthik Radhakrishnan, an MS Graduate from the University of Southern California.

Observing the huge unorganised sector in the interior design industry which led to inefficient products and inconvenienced customers, Niche was set up to solve these issues.
